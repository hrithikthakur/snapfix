Welcome to SnapFix

Fix your text anywhere with one simple shortcut

⌥ + Space

Press ⌥ Space (Alt+Space) to fix selected text instantly in any app.

  1. Select any text in any app
  2. Press ⌥ Space (Alt+Space)
  3. Your text is fixed instantly!

Enable permissions

SnapFix needs Accessibility permissions to replace text in other apps.

⚙️
System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility
SnapFix runs quietly in the background. Select text anywhere and press Alt + Space (⌥ Space on macOS) to fix it instantly. Use the tray icon to manage permissions or quit the app.
